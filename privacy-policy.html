Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) or Usage Data. You do not need to create an account to use the Application.

Device Permissions

While using Our Application, we require specific permissions to perform the core function of the app (Optical Character Recognition). These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the app and not for data collection.

1. Camera Access

The Application requires access to your Device's camera to capture images of documents or text for OCR processing. Images captured are processed instantly to extract text and are not uploaded to Our servers unless explicitly required for a specific cloud-processing feature you initiate. Typically, processing is done locally on your device.

2. Photo Library / Gallery Access

The Application requires access to your photo library to allow you to select existing images for text extraction. We only access the specific images you select.
